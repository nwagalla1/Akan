This page allows you to know your Akan Name. You're probably wondering the meaning of Akan Names. Right?

Akan names are derived from the Ghananian Culture. With Akan names, a new-born's first name is derived from the day of the week they were born, male and females are assigned separate names depending on their gender. Sounds Cool huh! Fill the form on the right to know your Akan name.
